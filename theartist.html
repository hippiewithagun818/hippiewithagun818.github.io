Ford Photographie

I am a native Los Angelino. Since I was a small child I've longed to create, I knew that I wanted to be a photographer. All of the photographs I took as a child have been lost to time. I'm good at what I do and far from success. I can't give up photography even if I wanted. The photojournalist in me loves to shoot street photography, capturing life, real moments as they are happening. The artist in me loves shooting fashion and artistic nudes, in the studio and in nature. Today I'm working on becoming a Front-End Web Developer.
